- Traditional web hosts (GoDaddy, Bluehost, etc.)

CUSTOM DOMAIN SETUP:
-------------------
To point www.chancewemeet.com to your hosting:
1. Get your host's DNS settings (IP address or CNAME)
2. Go to your domain registrar (where you bought chancewemeet.com)
3. Update DNS records to point to your hosting provider
4. Wait 5-60 minutes for DNS propagation
